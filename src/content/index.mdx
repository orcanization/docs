# Welcome to Orca

Orca(nization) is a tool for managing and organizing your projects, tasks, and resources. It provides a centralized platform for collaboration and communication, allowing teams to work together efficiently and effectively.

Orca is built on purpose with a minimal feature set, allowing users to customize and extend the platform to fit their specific needs.

<Callout type="warning">
    Orca is currently in early development and may not be suitable for production use. But our goal is to provide a detailed documentation from the beginning to help users understand the platform and contribute to its development.
</Callout>

## Goals
- Provide the needed base features to manage projects, tasks, and resources (Users, Projects, Tasks, Permission)
- Make it easy to extend the platform with custom features and integrations
- Provide a detailed documentation to help users understand the platform and contribute to its development
- Let other developers build plugins which can be easily shared and installed by other users
